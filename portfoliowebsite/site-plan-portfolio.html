Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my portfolio website will serve as a centralized hub showcasing my diverse range of projects, providing potential employers with a clear and concise view of my skills and experience. Each project will be presented with detailed descriptions, visuals, and links to live demos or repositories, allowing employers to directly assess my technical abilities and creative approach. Integrated with my online resume, the website will offer a comprehensive overview of my qualifications, making it easy for employers to evaluate my fit for their open positions.

Audience

My portfolio website is primarily targeted towards potential employers in the software development industry who are seeking skilled and creative professionals. It also serves as a platform to connect with other professionals in my field and showcase my work to a broader audience interested in software development.
